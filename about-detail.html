My Story

Developer, Designer, and Lifelong Learner

Who Am I?

Hi! I’m Arbab Nasrullah, an 18 year old developer, designer, trader, and entrepreneur from Peshawar, Pakistan.

I completed my matriculation in Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics) from Hira Public School Shahalam Pull, Peshawar (2021–2023), and currently pursuing ICS Computer Science at Government College Peshawar (2023–2025). Inshallah, I plan to apply for a bachelor’s degree this year.

I am the founder and CEO of two brands:

  • Who Cares ANK started in 2024, focused on brand building and community projects.
  • Unreal Trader started in 2022, blending technology with trading and financial education.

Alongside my brands, I work on E-commerce projects, App/web Development, and Trading. I am passionate about sharing knowledge for free through social media platforms like Facebook, WhatsApp, and Telegram, so anyone can learn and earn without fees or restrictions.

My journey started with a love for gaming (Free Fire, PUBG) from 2019 to late 2021, but I decided to shift to real life opportunities. I began with TikTok and Facebook marketing, then explored Ethical Hacking for several months, and finally found my focus in Trading. I launched a free WhatsApp channel to share courses and tips, and now I’m expanding my reach across more platforms.

My mission is simple: Learn, Build, Share help others grow. I’m proud to be a Pathan who believes in hard work, continuous growth, and giving back to the community.

If you want to join me on this journey, follow my channels and start your learning and earning today!

My Journey

2021: The Spark

In 2021, I completed my Matric in simple science from Hira Public School, Shahalam Pull, Peshawar. This was the foundation year when I started realizing my passion for technology and development. Although I was more into gaming at that time, I slowly became curious about how apps and websites were built. This curiosity sparked my journey into learning basic computer skills and opening doors to future tech opportunities.

2023: Learning & Exploring Building Blocks

In 2023, I joined Government College Peshawar to study ICS with Computer Science. Here, I started seriously learning programming languages, basic coding, and software development. Alongside, I also explored trading and hacking for a few months to understand the digital world better. This year was about experimenting, failing, and learning from mistakes. I also created my first small projects, which gave me confidence and helped me understand the tech ecosystem.

2023: First Projects & First Success Turning Ideas Into Reality

By 2023, I was more focused on building real projects and applying what I learned. I launched my own brand Unreal Trader focused on trading and financial education. I also started working on E-commerce projects and App & Web Development. My Trading journey started giving small profits, which motivated me to keep going. This was the year I launched free courses and educational content on social media platforms like Facebook and WhatsApp to help others learn without any cost or restrictions.

2024: The Future is Now

In 2024, I took a big leap by becoming the Founder & CEO of Who Cares ANK , a brand dedicated to empowering learners through technology and trading. Alongside this, I launched a laptop selling website project focused on providing affordable, quality laptops to students and professionals in Peshawar. This year marked the expansion of my vision combining entrepreneurship, e-commerce, and tech development to build impactful digital solutions.

2025: Vision & Growth

In 2025, I plan to apply for a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science to deepen my technical knowledge. I aim to become a full-stack developer specializing in e-commerce, app development, and trading platforms. My mission is to create scalable projects and provide free education and tools to help others succeed digitally. This year is all about growth, learning, and shaping a future where technology empowers everyone around me.
